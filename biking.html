my story:

Mountain biking has been a passion of mine since childhood. What began with simple, awe-inspiring rides alongside my dad quickly evolved into a fierce dedication to the sport. My journey led me to compete in Utah NICA races and the I-Cup series, where I discovered my intensely competitive nature and unwavering drive to excel. This determination has propelled me from being a young enthusiast to becoming a proud member of Accel Cycling's 2025 national team.

I’ve competed in Advanced Boys and Freshman A categories in NICA races, stood on podiums at I-Cup events, and challenged myself at iconic races like the Sea Otter Classic. Recently, I achieved a top-five finish at Frog Hollow solo, a testament to my relentless work ethic and resilience.

This season, I’m pushing even harder, redefining my limits and striving for new heights. My goals include competing as a Cat-1 rider in prestigious USA Cycling events such as the Orange Seal Pro Cup in Temecula, CA, and the XCO in Missoula, MT. Additionally, I plan to tackle USA-sanctioned Intermountain Cup races, the Sea Otter Classic in Monterey, and the True Grit Epic. Each race represents an opportunity to elevate my performance, embrace the challenge, and leave my mark on the sport I love.
